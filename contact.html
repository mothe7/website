Common Questions

How do I place an order?

Call or WhatsApp us at 94881 41979, or fill in the contact form on this page. We'll confirm your order and delivery details directly.

Is product customization available?

Yes! Product customization is available as per your preference. Tell us what you need and we'll prepare it fresh, just for you.

Do your products contain preservatives?

No — never. Every product we make is 100% free from artificial preservatives, chemicals, and additives. Natural only.

Where are you located?

We are based in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu, India. We make all products in our home kitchen here and deliver locally and beyond.

What are your business hours?

Monday–Friday 9am–5pm, Saturday 10am–3pm. We are closed on Sundays. WhatsApp messages are read during working hours.

Can I order in bulk?

Absolutely. We accept bulk orders for all products. Contact us directly to discuss quantities, pricing, and delivery timelines.
